About this form

ASSA is a research project building the Japanese Dysarthria Speech Corpus (JDSC) — a voice corpus for people with dysarthric speech. Your support helps us expand the corpus, compensate speakers, and present at international venues.

After submitting this form, our representative (SAI LLC / Go Suenaga) will reply by email to discuss donation methods, tax treatment, receipts, and any questions on a case-by-case basis.
